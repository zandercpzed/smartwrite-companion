We've ditched the cloud. SmartWrite now integrates directly with **Ollama**, allowing you to run powerful models like **Qwen 2.5** entirely on your machine.

- **Privacy First**: Your manuscript never leaves your computer.

### 2. Longform Plugin Integration üìö

This is the game-changer for novelists.

- **Project-Wide Analysis**: SmartWrite now detects your **Longform** projects automatically.
- **Stitched Context**: It reads your scene order (from the Longform index) and stitches them together into a single context for the AI.
- **Result**: You can ask the AI to analyze your _entire_ book for plot holes, pacing, or character consistency, not just one file at a time.

### 3. Advanced Readability & Stats üìä

- **Real-time Analysis**: Passive voice, adverbs, and complex sentence detection as you type.

- **Session Tracking**: Set daily word count goals and track your writing speed.
